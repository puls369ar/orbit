# Market
The indoor AMR market is a high-velocity play, scaling to $81 billion by 2031 with a 26.9% CAGR. For volume, Asia-Pacific dominates, holding 45% of the market; China’s 90% supply chain localization makes it the global leader in cost-efficient production. For growth momentum, the Middle East is the premier frontier, boasting the world’s highest CAGR at 28.7% due to state-backed logistics mandates like Saudi Vision 2030. Investors should prioritize regions pivoting toward Robots-as-a-Service models, which convert high upfront costs into stable, recurring revenue. The strongest metrics are found where labor shortages meet aggressive infrastructure subsidies.
